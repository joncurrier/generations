Basil Pesto

A fresh-tasting sauce made from basil leaves that goes well with pasta, bread, tomatoes, and seafood.

Yields: 1 pint

Prep: 10 minutes

Ingredients

3 ounces fresh basil leaves
2 ounces Parmesan (grated)
3 ounces pine nuts (toasted)
1/2 cups olive oil
1 teaspoon lemon zest
1 clove garlic

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a vigorous boil, then blanche the basil leaves for thirty seconds. Immediately transfer them to a bowl of ice water.
  2. Squeeze the basil leaves dry using paper towels, then combine all the ingredients in a food processor and blend on high speed until desired consistency is reached.
