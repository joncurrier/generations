Three Cheese Hot Artichoke Dip

This is a Paula Deen recipe, and as such it's as delicious as it is unhealthy.

Yields: 3 to 4 servings

Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 30 minutes

Ingredients

8 ounces cream cheese (softened)
2 cups mayonnaise
1 can artichoke hearts
2 green onions
1/2 cup Parmesan (grated)
1 cup mozzarella (shredded)
1 teaspoon hot sauce
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Drain and chop the artichoke hearts.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese with an electric hand mixer until smooth.
  4. Add the mayonnaise and beat until smooth.
  5. Add the remaining ingredients and stir to combine.
  6. Transfer the dip to a pie plate or shallow gratin dish.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the dip is bubbling.
