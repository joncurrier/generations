Brownie Cookies

A small-batch recipe for gluten-free (but not sugar-free!) chocolate brownie cookies.

Yields: 10-12 cookies

Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 17 minutes

Ingredients

100 grams powdered sugar
37 grams unsweetened cocoa powder
2 large egg whites
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon salt
3 tablespoons chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, cocoa powder, and salt until combined. Make sure no clumps remain.
  3. In another medium bowl, using an electric hand mixer on high speed, beat the egg whites and vanilla together until soft peaks form.
  4. One half at a time, pour the solid mixture into the egg whites and gently fold together until moistened and incorporated, taking care not to overmix.
  5. Gently mix in the chocolate chips.
  6. Spoon the batter in ten to twelve equal sized portions onto a cookie sheet lined with wax paper.
  7. Bake for 17 minutes or until the tops of the cookies are dry and the edges have just begun to crack.
  8. Remove from oven and let cool on the cookie sheet for five minutes, then transfer to a cooling rack.
